Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I submit an assistance request?

Log into the Citizen Portal, click "Submit New Request," fill out the form with your needs, and submit. You'll receive a confirmation and tracking number immediately.

How long does it take to get a response?

Response times vary based on severity. Critical requests are addressed within 2 hours, urgent within 6 hours, and standard requests within 24-48 hours.

Can I track my request status?

Yes! Log into your Citizen Portal dashboard to view real-time updates on all your requests, including status changes and assigned personnel.

Is my personal information secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and follow strict government data protection protocols to ensure your information remains private and secure.

What if I don't have internet access?

You can call our hotline at +1 (555) 123-4567 or visit our office in person. Our staff will help you submit your request.
